## What the website does

The site serves three audiences:

1. **Prospective parents and players** — searching for a local club, comparing options, deciding whether to register
2. **Existing members** — checking fixtures, results, team news, and booking the astro pitch
3. **Volunteers and sponsors** — finding out how to get involved or partner with the club

It is built to work on all devices, is fully accessible, and updates in real time for fixtures and league tables. It is designed to compete with the best club websites in Europe — not just locally.

The site currently scores well technically but lacks one thing: **real people**. Photos, real names on the safeguarding page, training schedules per team, and a general contact number. Those four things are the difference between a prospective parent clicking "Register" and clicking away.
